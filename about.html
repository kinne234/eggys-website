About Eggy's

Eggy's is an urban, hip, neighborhood café serving all-day breakfast, lunch, family-style dinner & baked goods made from seasonal, local ingredients. We want everyone to feel a sense of community while enjoying delicious food inspired by home-cooking.


While the name may conjur visions of breakfast, the restaurant is in fact named after one of the owner's childhood friends, Robert Mallin, who was nicknamed "EGGY" (after his self-described egg-shaped head) back in the late 1960's. Characterized by his friends as loyal, fair and philanthropic, Robert "Eggy" Mallin personifies what Eggy's is all about: family, friends and community.

Meet Eggy


Born: February 28, 1963 in Edgewater Hospital (Chicago, IL).


How did you get your nickname? I’ve always had a freakishly large head. Actually this caricature of me is drawn to scale. I’m basically a real life bobble-head doll.


Hobbies: I have completed 10 iron man races and over 30 Marathons, which is especially impressive when you consider that my giant egg-shaped melon isn’t very aerodynamic.


Passions: Family, fitness, and eating at Eggy’s (not necessarily in that order).


Random Thought: Humpty Dumpty was pushed!
